📅 Weekly Confidence Plan (4 Weeks)

    💪 Week 1 – I Can Try:
    Activity: “Bravery Jar” – Write down 1 thing you tried this week and share.
    Lesson: Everyone fails sometimes, but we grow when we try.
    🗣️ Week 2 – I Can Speak Up:
    Activity: “Talk Time” – Practice introducing yourself or asking a question.
    Lesson: Using your voice shows confidence.
    🪞 Week 3 – I Am Awesome:
    Activity: Make a “Confidence Mirror” and say something good about yourself.
    Lesson: Positive self-talk builds strength.
    🎯 Week 4 – I Can Reach My Goal:
    Activity: Set one personal goal (e.g., read daily) and track it.
    Lesson: Small wins = Big confidence!

🎉 End of Program Celebration

    🌟 Confidence Awards: Celebrate bravery, kindness, speaking up, and effort.
    🧁 Treats & Sharing: Students share 1 thing they’re proud of.
